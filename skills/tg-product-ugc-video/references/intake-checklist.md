# Intake Checklist

Use this checklist before generating the final UGC director package.

## Required Inputs

Ask for missing items in plain language:

1. Product material
   - Product image, product page, product name, or product description.
   - If only a white-background product image is provided, infer visible details and ask the user to confirm.

2. Target country or region
   - Let the user choose. Do not assume the market.
   - Use this to decide language, culture, platform habits, claims to avoid, and creator persona.

3. Publishing platform
   - TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, Amazon video, Douyin, Xiaohongshu, Taobao, Shopee, Rakuten, Qoo10, Mercado Libre, or other.
   - If the user is unsure, recommend based on country.

4. Video duration
   - 15 seconds: fast hook and one clear selling point.
   - 30 seconds: hook, pain point, demo, proof, CTA.
   - 45 seconds: fuller education, comparison, objection handling, CTA.

5. UGC style
   - User may choose from the style library.
   - If unsure, auto-select one primary style and one backup style.

6. Video language and tone
   - Match target country by default.
   - Ask when the market is multilingual or when the user wants a specific accent.

7. Creator type
   - Let the user choose, or auto-generate from product and audience.
   - Examples: beauty micro-influencer, busy mom, office worker, outdoor hobbyist, student, tech reviewer, fitness beginner.

8. Product selling points
   - Ask for official selling points if available.
   - If not available, infer from visible packaging/product type and mark as inferred.

9. Available assets
   - Product image only
   - Product image plus generated character image
   - Product image plus real footage
   - Product image plus brand guideline

10. Restrictions
   - Brand words to include or avoid
   - Claims that must not be made
   - Competitor names to avoid
   - Required CTA or store link wording

## Beginner-Friendly Question Block

When several items are missing, ask like this:

```text
我先补齐制作前信息，避免直接写成空泛文案：
1. 投放国家/地区是哪里？
2. 发布平台选哪个？
3. 视频做 15 秒、30 秒还是 45 秒？
4. 风格你想选哪种，还是让我按产品自动判断？
5. 产品卖点你有现成的吗？没有的话我先根据图片判断，再让你确认。
6. 达人类型有要求吗？没有的话我按目标用户自动设定。
```

## Defaults When User Says "You Decide"

- Platform: recommend based on country.
- Duration: 30 seconds for first version.
- Style: auto-select based on product category and buyer motivation.
- Language: match target country.
- Creator: micro-influencer style, realistic and non-advertising.
- Selling points: infer conservatively from product and ask for confirmation.
