# UGC Style Library

Use this library to select the best UGC style for the product, country, platform, and duration.

## Style Options

### 1. Real-Person Review

Use when:

- Product needs trust.
- Buyer wants another user's experience.
- Category includes beauty, skincare, home goods, gadgets, fitness, supplements, or daily-use products.

Structure:

Hook -> personal context -> product use -> result/feeling -> CTA.

### 2. Unboxing

Use when:

- Packaging, first impression, or giftability matters.
- Product is visually satisfying.
- Product can be shown step by step.

Structure:

Hook -> package reveal -> what's inside -> first use -> quick opinion -> CTA.

### 3. Pain-Point Rant

Use when:

- The product solves an obvious frustration.
- Buyer already knows the problem.
- The product has a simple before/after benefit.

Structure:

Complaint -> show problem -> introduce product -> quick demo -> relief -> CTA.

### 4. Before/After Comparison

Use when:

- Difference can be shown visually.
- Product effect is immediate or demonstrable.
- Good for cleaning, beauty, organization, lighting, accessories, and tool products.

Structure:

Before -> problem detail -> product use -> after -> comparison text -> CTA.

Avoid unsupported medical, body transformation, or guaranteed result claims.

### 5. Casual Selfie

Use when:

- Product should feel native to TikTok/Reels.
- Creator trust matters more than polished production.
- Audience dislikes obvious ads.

Structure:

Casual confession -> show product -> one honest reason -> quick demo -> soft CTA.

### 6. Influencer Recommendation

Use when:

- Product is aesthetic, premium, trend-driven, or lifestyle-oriented.
- The audience responds to taste, status, or social proof.

Structure:

Trend/opinion hook -> product endorsement -> why it fits -> usage scene -> CTA.

### 7. Mini Drama

Use when:

- Product solves a relatable daily conflict.
- The audience enjoys entertainment-first content.
- Works better for 30s or 45s than 15s.

Structure:

Conflict -> awkward moment -> product appears -> resolution -> punchline/CTA.

## Auto-Selection Rules

- Beauty and skincare: casual selfie, real-person review, before/after.
- Home cleaning and organization: pain-point rant, before/after, mini drama.
- 3C and gadgets: real-person review, problem-solution demo, unboxing.
- Fashion and accessories: influencer recommendation, casual selfie, unboxing.
- Kitchen and household tools: pain-point rant, demo review, before/after.
- Pet products: mini drama, real-person review, before/after.
- Fitness products: real-person review, before/after with cautious claims.
- Gifts and novelty items: unboxing, influencer recommendation, mini drama.

## Duration Fit

- 15s: pain-point rant, before/after, casual selfie.
- 30s: review, unboxing, influencer recommendation.
- 45s: mini drama, detailed review, education plus demo.

## Platform Fit

- TikTok: casual selfie, pain-point rant, mini drama, fast review.
- Instagram Reels: aesthetic recommendation, unboxing, lifestyle demo.
- YouTube Shorts: clearer explanation, review, comparison.
- Xiaohongshu: experience sharing, ingredient/material explanation, lifestyle use.
- Douyin: strong hook, fast demo, pain-point or transformation.
- Marketplace video: direct demo, clear feature proof, low entertainment overhead.
