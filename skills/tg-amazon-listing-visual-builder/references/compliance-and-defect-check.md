# Compliance And Defect Check

## Compliance Principles

Check every image before calling it finished. Be conservative when the product touches health, safety, children, pets, food contact, electronics, cosmetics, or regulated claims.

Avoid:

- Unsupported medical, therapeutic, safety, or performance claims.
- Absolute words such as "best", "guaranteed", "100% safe", "cures", or "risk-free" unless properly substantiated and allowed.
- Fake ratings, fake reviews, fake discount tags, fake Amazon/TikTok UI, marketplace logos, or unauthorized trust badges.
- Certification marks, warranty badges, official seals, or compliance icons unless the user provides proof.
- Competitor logos, brand marks, packaging, or recognizable copied layouts.
- Misleading before/after results.
- Unrealistic size, quantity, included accessories, compatibility, or use context.
- Product use that looks unsafe, illegal, or culturally inappropriate for the target country.

## Main Image Specific Risks

For marketplace main images, watch for:

- Text or graphics when the platform/category prohibits them.
- Props that imply included items not sold with the product.
- Backgrounds that violate category expectations.
- Cropped product edges that hide true form.
- Watermarks, QR codes, promotional stickers, or social handles.

If rules are uncertain, mark the image as "needs platform-rule confirmation" rather than upload-ready.

## AI Defect Prevention

Every prompt and review should prevent:

- Product deformation.
- Product or hands passing through each other.
- Distorted fingers, faces, wrists, or body posture.
- Warped packaging, unreadable labels, fake brand text, or broken typography.
- Mismatched product color, size, material, or accessory count.
- Floating products, impossible shadows, wrong reflections, or inconsistent perspective.
- Duplicate products when only one is sold.
- Extra cables, buttons, seams, textures, or labels not present in the source product.
- Background clutter that competes with the product.
- Overly glossy plastic look when the material should be matte, fabric, metal, wood, or glass.

## Required Negative Prompt

Adapt this negative prompt for every image:

No product deformation, no inaccurate SKU color, no extra accessories, no missing accessories, no floating objects, no impossible perspective, no warped packaging text, no fake logo, no marketplace badge, no fake certification, no fake reviews, no exaggerated claim text, no body-object intersection, no hands passing through product, no malformed fingers, no distorted face, no duplicated product, no low-resolution texture, no messy background, no unreadable small text, no unsafe use scene.

## Final Review Labels

Use one of these labels:

- Upload-ready: enough source material, clean visual, low compliance risk.
- Needs manual rule check: likely usable but platform/category rule may be stricter.
- Needs source asset: exact product appearance cannot be preserved.
- Needs manual retouch: design is useful but AI defects or text rendering risks remain.
- Not recommended: compliance or misrepresentation risk is too high.
